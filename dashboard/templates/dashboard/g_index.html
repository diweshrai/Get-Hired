Placements Overview

Fantasizing of working with Fortune 500, many reputed national and multinational companies visits GEC for campus recruitment annually because of our high qualityskilled talents with best in industry communication skill sets. On an average, 80 to 90% of eligible students are placed in last 7 years. Commitment to create talent job ready based on strong Industry-Academic interface, GEC happens to be setting new benchmarks in campus placements. Year after year top notch companies such as Wipro, Tech Mahindra, R1 RCM, BYJU’S, UST Global, Schindler Group, IBM, Motherson Sumi, VIVO Mobiles are visiting GEC Campus for recruitment of fresh talent. As a result, GEC has been awarded with the title “ ASSOCHAM Best Placement Award”
